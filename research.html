Project 01 2025.11-Present

Scheduling of Single-Arm Multicluster Tools for Multi-Type Wafers with Parallel Processing, Residency Time Constraints, and Reentrant Buffer Cooling Operations

Event-Driven Simulation Safety Shielding Joint-Action Certification Cooling Reentry RTSS 2026 Under Review
Time2025.11-Present
RoleProject Lead

Problem: Schedule single-arm multicluster tools under cooling reentry, shared-buffer direction constraints, cooling readiness, residency-time limits, and robot joint-action coupling.

Method: A structure-guided, constraint-aware multi-option scheduler with runtime safety shielding and joint-action certification.

Progress: Event-driven simulation, constraint checking, adversarial unsafe proposal testing, ablation experiments, and response-latency statistics support the RTSS 2026 under-review paper.

My Contribution: I led cooling-reentry scenario construction, simulation and constraint checking, scheduling framework design, safety-shield implementation, experiment design, and paper writing.

First author-IEEE Real-Time Systems Symposium (RTSS 2026)-CCF-A conference

Project 02 2025.06-2026.03

Scheduling of Single-Arm Multicluster Tools for Multi-Type Wafers with Parallel Processing and Residency Time Constraints

Multi-Agent RL MA-QMIX-PPO Residency Constraints Dynamic Feasibility Masks Benchmark Evaluation T-ASE Under Review CCF-B Journal
Time2025.06-2026.03
RoleProject Lead / First Author

Problem: Coordinate multi-type wafer flows with parallel processing, shared modules, buffer competition, residency-time constraints, and deadlock risk.

Method: MA-QMIX-PPO with dynamic feasibility masks, centralized monotonic value decomposition, PPO joint-action refinement, expert-guided imitation, successful-trajectory replay, and elite archive search.

Progress: Baseline and CP-SAT comparisons across 51 perturbation cases support the first-author IEEE T-ASE under-review manuscript.

My Contribution: I led problem modeling, MDP definition, event-driven simulation, robot coordination interfaces, algorithm design, experiment organization, result analysis, and manuscript writing.

First author-IEEE Transactions on Automation Science and Engineering-SCI Q1, CCF-B journal

Project 03 2025.06-2026.03

Scheduling of a Single-Arm Cluster Tool for Processing Two Types of Wafers with Parallel Processing

Deep RL Improved TD3 Cluster Tool CSCWD 2026 Accepted
Time2025.06-2026.03
RoleProject Lead / First Author

Problem: Optimize full-cycle scheduling for a single-arm cluster tool processing two wafer types.

Method: An MDP formulation solved by improved TD3 with legal-action handling, reward reconstruction, dynamic exploration, prioritized replay, cyclic learning rate, attention, and normalized states.

Progress: Experiments show lower makespan, faster convergence, and stronger robustness than TD3, DDPG, and DQN; the first-author CSCWD 2026 paper has been accepted.

My Contribution: I handled problem modeling, MDP definition, simulation environment programming, legal-action and constraint-checking implementation, algorithm design, comparison experiments, result analysis, and main paper writing.

First author-CSCWD 2026, a CCF-C conference-CCF-C conference

Project 04 2024.09-2025.07

Intelligent Scheduling System for Central Air Conditioning

Transformer Prediction DDPG HVAC Control Power Regulation TII Under Review PRICAI Under Review Regional Third Prize
Time2024.09-2025.07
RoleProject Lead / Third Author

Problem: Improve coordinated scheduling of central air-conditioning equipment under dynamic load, environmental temperature changes, and operating constraints.

Method: Neural-network and Transformer-based power prediction combined with DDPG continuous-action adaptive scheduling.

Progress: The project produced an adaptive HVAC scheduling prototype, won Third Prize in the Guangdong Regional Competition of the China Robot and Artificial Intelligence Competition, and supports under-review TII and PRICAI 2026 papers.

My Contribution: I led data modeling, power-prediction model training, and intelligent control algorithm implementation.

Third author-IEEE Transactions on Industrial Informatics-SCI Q1, CCF-B journal; Second author-PRICAI 2026-CCF-C conference
